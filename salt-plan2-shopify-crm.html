01 What Is Plan 2?

Plan 2 takes everything Plan 1 achieves ‚Äî unified order intake, unread email alerts, department routing, and real-time status tracking ‚Äî and builds it on a more powerful foundation: Shopify as the operational core connected to a dedicated CRM (Customer Relationship Management) platform.

Instead of stitching together Zapier and Airtable, Plan 2 uses Shopify's native integrations and a CRM's built-in automation to handle order management, customer profiles, follow-ups, marketing, and reporting all in one place.

üîë The Core Difference

Plan 1 is an order tracker. Plan 2 is an order tracker + customer intelligence system. With a CRM, Salt Lagos gains full visibility into who their customers are ‚Äî their order history, preferences, lifetime spend, and how they were acquired ‚Äî enabling smarter marketing, personalised follow-ups, and loyalty building at scale.

‚úî What Plan 2 Adds Over Plan 1

05 Automation Flows (What Happens Automatically)

Plan 2's biggest advantage over Plan 1 is the depth and intelligence of automation. The CRM can trigger personalised, context-aware actions based on customer behaviour and order data ‚Äî without manual intervention.

Order Management Automations

  • ‚ÜíNew Shopify order received: CRM creates order record, notifies Sales/Admin in the CRM dashboard, sends internal WhatsApp alert to team, triggers customer confirmation email.
  • ‚ÜíUnread email order detected: CRM flags email as unread ticket, sends alert to Sales inbox, logs a task for follow-up within 2 hours. Auto-acknowledgement sent to customer.
  • ‚ÜíOrder confirmed by Sales: CRM triggers notification to Kitchen team with order details, flavour notes, and required completion date. Status updates to "In Production."
  • ‚ÜíOrder dispatched: Customer automatically receives a WhatsApp or email message with delivery update and care instructions (refrigerate immediately, consume within 1 week).
  • ‚ÜíOrder completed: CRM logs completion, updates customer lifetime value, and optionally triggers a feedback/review request 24 hours later.

Customer Marketing Automations

  • ‚òÖBirthday campaign: If a customer's birthday is on record, CRM sends them a "Treat yourself to a Salt cake this birthday üç∞" email 1 week in advance with a direct link to the Shopify store.
  • ‚òÖWin-back sequence: If a customer hasn't ordered in 60 days, CRM automatically sends a personalised re-engagement email: "We miss you at Salt Lagos ‚Äî here's what's new on our menu."
  • ‚òÖPost-event follow-up: After an events order (weddings, corporate), CRM sends a follow-up 2 weeks later asking for feedback and referrals.
  • ‚òÖSeasonal promotions: CRM segments customers (e.g. those who ordered in December last year) and sends targeted campaigns for Christmas, Valentine's Day, Easter orders.
  • ‚òÖSocial media lead nurture: When a social enquiry is converted to a contact but hasn't ordered yet, CRM drip-sends 2‚Äì3 emails over 1 week showcasing Salt's menu and testimonials.
